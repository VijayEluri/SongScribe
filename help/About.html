Welcome to the SongScribe!

This program designed for Maestro Sri Chinmoy's songs. You can edit songs and then play it back, print, export and store. Therefore this is an excellent choice to those who are involved in Maestro Sri Chinmoy's music.
This software pack also includes a slide show maker and a song-publication maker.

Features:

  • Edit, play back and print music in the Sri Chinmoy's music conventions.
  • Supports 128 instrument on playback.
  • Export music as MIDI music file.
  • Export music as image (GIF, JPEG, PNG, BMP).
  • Export music as PDF for high quality use.
  • Supports editing with mouse and keyboard.
  • A lot of view-adjustment of music.
  • Supports lyrics on high level.

SongScribe Song Show features:

  • Displays and plays back songs in full-screen mode.
  • Stores assemble of songs for slide show.

SongScribe Song Book features:

  • Creates a document (song book) from assemble of songs, text and images.
  • Tools for moving, resizing, formating objects.
  • Various page-layouts.
  • Export as high quality PDF for printing or press use.
