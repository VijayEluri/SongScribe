What's new in SongScribe 1.20

  • On Retina displays, all interface elements except toolbar buttons are now displayed in full Retina resolution
  • When you open a song file, the window automatically resizes to fit its width
  • Pressing Space will once again start/pause playback
  • Command/Control-period stops playback
  • Fixed alignment of note stems
  • Fixed alignment of flags

What's new in SongScribe 1.19

  • Crescendo & Diminuendo
  • Vertically adjustable triplets and tublets
  • The long hyphen is now horizontally adjustable with Lyrics adjustments
  • In print view, putting the augmentation dot further from 8th, 16th and 32nd note, not to touch the flag
  • Possibility to invert the orientation of fractional beams
  • New letters in lyrics dialog
  • Possibility to add lyrics syllables for rests
  • In print view, the accidentals are now smaller for grace notes
  • On the toolbar now all the four adjustment modes are available
  • Several bug fixes for long dashes and extender lines in lyrics
  • Fixing a bug which prevented music-repaint after inserting a glissando
